Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

In short: LockRoute Pro uses only essential cookies and local storage required to run the app. We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Your data stays yours.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LockRoute Pro ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies when you visit our website at lockroutepro.com or use our web application.

This policy complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR).

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owners. Local storage and session storage are similar browser technologies that allow websites to store data on your device.

3. How We Use Cookies and Storage

LockRoute Pro uses only strictly necessary cookies and storage mechanisms that are essential for the app to function. We do not use any cookies for advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking purposes.

3.1 Categories of Cookies We Use

Our cookies and storage fall into two categories:

  • Essential cookies — required for authentication and core app functionality. These cannot be disabled as the app will not work without them.
  • Functional storage — used to cache data locally so the app can work offline and load faster. These improve performance but do not track you.

4. Cookie and Storage Details

The table below lists every cookie and storage item used by LockRoute Pro, along with its purpose and how long it lasts.

Name Type Purpose Duration Category
sb-*-auth-token Cookie Supabase authentication session token. Keeps you signed in securely. Session / up to 7 days Essential
sb-*-auth-token-code-verifier Cookie PKCE code verifier for secure OAuth authentication flows. Session Essential
supabase.auth.token localStorage Stores the authentication refresh token to maintain your login session. Persistent (until logout) Essential
lr-jobs-cache localStorage Caches your job data locally so the app can load quickly and work offline. Persistent (until cleared) Functional
lr-calendar-cache localStorage Caches calendar and scheduling data for offline access. Persistent (until cleared) Functional
lr-settings localStorage Stores your app preferences such as home location, working hours, and display settings. Persistent (until cleared) Functional
lr-route-cache localStorage Caches route and geocoding data to reduce map API calls and improve speed. Persistent (until cleared) Functional
mapbox-gl-* Cookie / localStorage Set by Mapbox to deliver map tiles, handle map interactions, and cache map data for performance. Session / Persistent Functional

5. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use any third-party advertising, remarketing, or behavioural tracking cookies. The only third-party technology that may set cookies is Mapbox, which is used solely to provide map and routing functionality within the app. Mapbox processes data in accordance with their privacy policy.

6. Consent and Strictly Necessary Cookies

Under PECR and UK GDPR, strictly necessary cookies do not require consent. All cookies and storage used by LockRoute Pro fall into the "strictly necessary" or "functional" categories — they are essential for the app to operate or to provide features you have requested.

Because we do not use any advertising or non-essential tracking cookies, we do not display a cookie consent banner. If we ever introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and implement an appropriate consent mechanism before doing so.

7. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from signing in to LockRoute Pro.

To manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

To clear local storage data, you can use your browser's developer tools (usually accessible via F12) or clear all site data through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or regulation. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
